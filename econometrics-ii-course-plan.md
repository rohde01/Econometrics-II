## Course Topics Summary

The course covers the following major topics in econometrics:

1. **Likelihood Analysis** - Fundamentals of likelihood-based estimation
2. **Dynamic Models** - Time series models including AR, ARMA, ARIMA
3. **Vector Autoregressions (VAR)** - Multivariate dynamic models
4. **Structural VARs (SVAR)** - Identifying structural relationships
5. **Non-stationarity** - Unit root testing and distributional theory
6. **Co-integration** - Long-run equilibrium relationships in VAR frameworks
7. **ARCH/GARCH Models** - Conditional heteroskedasticity and volatility modeling
8. **Generalized Method of Moments (GMM)** - Alternative estimation approach with instrumental variables
